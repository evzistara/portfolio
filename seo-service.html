What is SEO

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. The purpose of SEO is to helps search engines understand your website's content and help you to rank on the first page of search engine result pages (SERPs) for the most relevant and valuable keywords.


This can be achieved by working on your onpage SEO (content on the website, articles, keywords, meta data,...), technical SEO (indexing of your pages, correct redirects,...) and off-page SEO (links from other websites).


It is important to note, that to see effects of SEO can take time (between 3 - 6 months). So don't get discouraged ☺
